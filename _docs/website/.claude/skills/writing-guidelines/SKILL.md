# CraigVan Writing Guidelines

**Purpose:** Maintain consistent brand voice across all written content
**Domain:** Content creation, copywriting, brand messaging
**Status:** Complete

---

## When to Use This Skill

**Trigger keywords:**
- ANY writing task longer than 100 words
- Editing existing content for voice
- Reviewing drafts for brand alignment

**When NOT to use:**
- Technical documentation (different voice)
- Code comments (different context)

---

## Core Voice Principles

### Who We're Talking To

You're speaking to yourself. The man who knows his food matters far beyond weight-loss.

Write like you're talking to your best friend who's struggling with the same stuff you've wrestled with. No preaching. Just truth from someone who's been there.

### How We Sound

Direct. Like a coach who gives a damn. Like a brother who won't let you settle.

Every sentence does work. Cut everything else. If you can't read it aloud without gasping for air, your reader can't either.

Talk to "you." Share what "I've learned." Build "we're in this together" moments.

Challenge, then lift. Show the problem, then show the way through.

---

## How to Write

Every word works or gets cut. One big idea per piece. Go deep, not wide.

Read everything aloud. If you gasp, they gasp.

### Kill the Clutter

**Cut these words:**
- "somewhat," "pretty much," "kind of" - they weaken you
- "It's interesting to note" and "What I want to say is"
- Turn concepts into actions: "optimize" → "improve," "implement" → "do"

### Make Science Human

| Health Jargon | Human Translation |
|---------------|-------------------|
| "Oxidative stress" | "your cells rusting from the inside" |
| "Inflammation" | "your immune system attacking your own tissue" |
| "Studies show" | "researchers followed 1,000 men and found..." |
| "Micronutrient deficiency" | "your body is starving for zinc" |
| "Optimize gut microbiome" | "feed the good bacteria in your gut" |
| "Enhance mitochondrial function" | "give your cells more energy" |
| "Reduce systemic inflammation" | "cool down the fire inside your body" |
| "Support circadian rhythm" | "sync your body clock with the sun" |
| "Improve insulin sensitivity" | "help your body handle sugar better" |

### Structure That Transforms

1. Hook with pain they feel right now
2. Show them where they are (external control) and where they could be (internal control)
3. Give them the core information
4. Paint the vision of their transformed life

---

## Say It Like a Human

**The coffee test:** Would you say this to a friend over coffee? If no, simplify.

### Natural Language Wins

**Use:**
- "Stop following someone else's diet" ✓
- "Your body knows what it needs" ✓
- "That pain is trying to tell you something" ✓
- "Work that actually matters to you" ✓

**Avoid the woo:**
- "Transform from Puppet to Pilot" ✗
- "Cultivate your inner gnosis" ✗
- "Embody your sacred masculinity" ✗
- "The Sovereign Man's journey" ✗

---

## Pre-Publish Checklist

Before publishing, check these boxes:

- [ ] One big idea you can say in 10 words
- [ ] Reads aloud without gasping
- [ ] Every word earns its place
- [ ] Opens with real pain, not generic fluff
- [ ] Passes the coffee test
- [ ] Includes your story or discovery
- [ ] Shows the path from controlled to controlling
- [ ] Gives them the next step
- [ ] No "maybe," "perhaps," "might," "kind of"
- [ ] Active voice throughout
- [ ] No em dashes (use periods or commas)

## Quick Reference

| Element | Requirement |
