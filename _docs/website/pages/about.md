## THE PATH

### Section: The Journey That Led Here

**Why Listen to Me?**

Not because I've mastered everything. Because I've walked a specific path. The cross-pollination of these domains created something I couldn't have planned.

---

### 2009-2011 | Mechanical Engineering
**University of the Witwatersrand, Johannesburg**

Three years of engineering fundamentals: physics, mechanics, thermodynamics, systems thinking. This taught me to see the world as interconnected systems with inputs, outputs, and feedback loops.

**What it gave me:** First principles thinking. Everything can be modeled as a system.

---

### 2012-2015 | Biomedical Sciences + Biokinetics Honours
**South Africa**

Early life crisis. Realized I'd never looked at a cell under a microscope. Pivoted completely.

Two years identical to medical doctors: anatomy, physiology, cadaver dissection. Then specialized in exercise science, therapeutic sciences, rehabilitation. Honours in Biokinetics (South African medical profession - movement rehabilitation).

**What it gave me:** Deep understanding of how bodies actually work. Not theory - the messy reality.

---

### 2016-2020 | Registered Biokinetician
**Own Practice, South Africa**

Five years as a registered health practitioner. Thousands of patients. Specialized in back pain (worked in a chiropractor's building - 80% of referrals were spine injuries).

**The lesson:** Information isn't the problem. Implementation is. Most of us don't need more advice. We need better systems.

**What it gave me:** Clinical experience. Real-world evidence of what works and what doesn't.

---

### Ongoing | Vipassana Practice
**11 Retreats, 110+ Days Silent**

Read Deepak Chopra's "Buddha: A Story of Enlightenment." The thought: "Even if a fraction of what this book says is true, I need to be meditating yesterday."

Eleven 10-day silent retreats since age 21. Daily practice: at least 1 hour evening, 30 minutes morning.

**The honest acknowledgment:**
> It has not been a miracle cure. I've come to learn just how unethical I am, how self-destructive, how untruthful. These challenges I'm letting go of, learning, moving through - all related to the meditative journey.

**What it gave me:** Direct experience that awareness is foundational to everything else.

---

### 2020-2023 | Europe: Business + Data Science
**Netherlands**

Left South Africa. Business Administration, then pivoted to Data Science Masters for faster path to employment.

Proper statistics, quantitative analysis, Python, coding foundations. Gateway to AI/ML capability.

**What it gave me:** Technical tools. The ability to build systems, not just think about them.

---

### 2023-Present | Technical Support Engineer
**Adyen, Amsterdam**

Full-stack technical support at a fintech company. Product integration, troubleshooting, debugging complex systems.

Effectively a degree in software technology, integration, networking.

**What it gave me:** Systems debugging at scale. Understanding how complex systems fail and how to fix them.

---

### Now | Building neoMeta
**Life Operating System**

All of this converged. The engineering thinking. The body understanding. The meditation practice. The technical capability.

The amount of work now possible with leveraged AI systems - the stuff I can build to add value based on this accumulated knowledge - is what makes this the moment.
