## FRAME 1: THE TIME PREFERENCE THESIS

### Section: The Pattern Beneath All Good Choices

### The Discovery

After 15 years of studying health, wealth, and connection, I found one pattern that unifies everything:

> **All choices that lead to fulfillment share one pattern: low time preference decisions overcoming high time preference impulses.**

### High Time Preference

Immediate gratification. Short-lived satisfaction. Followed by destructive consequences.

| Domain | High Time Preference Choice |
|--------|----------------------------|
| Health | Junk food, skipping workouts, numbing with substances |
| Wealth | Debt, get-rich-quick schemes, avoiding hard work |
| Connection | Conflict avoidance, people-pleasing, superficial relationships |

The hit is immediate. The cost is delayed. But it always comes.

### Low Time Preference

Perceived immediate sacrifice. Sustainably compounding long-term benefit.

| Domain | Low Time Preference Choice |
|--------|---------------------------|
| Health | Whole foods, consistent movement, meditation practice |
| Wealth | Saving, skill development, meaningful work |
| Connection | Difficult conversations, authentic expression, deep investment |

The cost is immediate. The benefit is delayed. But it compounds.

### The Compound of Compounds

Here's where it gets interesting:

> We can take many complementary, synergistic, parallel, uplifting behaviors and have them compound over time AND over each other.

- Better nutrition → more energy for movement
- Better movement → clearer mind for meditation
- Clearer meditation → better decisions about food
- Better decisions → stronger relationships
- Stronger relationships → accountability for health

Each improvement cascades. The compound effect multiplies across domains. This creates **exponential transformation**.

### The Human Limitation

> "Smart people across history have said: we simply cannot easily understand compounding consequences."

We can't perceive the compound effect in the moment of sacrifice. The future benefit is abstract. The present cost is concrete.

This is where systems (and AI) become crucial - they can visualize, track, and surface the compound effects our brains discount.

### The Reframe

Low time preference isn't about sacrifice. It's about:
- Investment (neutral framing)
- Self-love (positive framing)
- Alignment with your future self (identity framing)

> "Delayed gratification" sounds like denial. "Living in integrity with your future self" sounds like wholeness.

We're getting our first taste of what these systems can do. Extremely powerful with seemingly unlimited potential. Even if models didn't improve from here, what we'll see unlocked as agentic architecture develops will be staggering.

Before someone else decides for us.

## FRAME 3: DECENTRALIZED ALIGNMENT

### Section: Who Gets To Define Your Highest Good?

### The Current Reality

Right now, AI alignment sits with a handful of corporations and governments. They're working on collective safety. Avoiding harm, being helpful, refusing dangerous requests.

This is important work. But it's not enough.

### The Vision

> Each individual should have equal power in defining their AI's alignment. Not corporations. Not governments. Each of us.

### The Dual Maximization

The AI that each of us individually interacts with must simultaneously:

1. **Maximize for individual highest good** - as we subjectively perceive and choose
2. **Maximize for collective highest good** - at ALL layers of the fractal (individual, family, community, country, globe)

These aren't in conflict. Your highest good, properly understood, includes the good of those around you.

### Two Layers of Alignment

**Layer 1: Foundation Models (Collective Safety)**
Big AI companies handle:
- Safety constraints
- Harm prevention
- General helpfulness
- Truthfulness

This is THEIR job.

**Layer 2: Personal Context (Individual Alignment)**
It's on US to tailor-fit these models to serve our unique needs:
- Context engineering (system prompts, memory, knowledge bases)
- Agentic architectures (multi-agent systems, specialized roles)
- Personal data integration
- Value alignment

**The Core Insight:**
> If we want AI that provides maximal benefit for us as individuals, we have to handle our own unique alignment challenges.

### Git Repos as Alignment Nodes

**The vision:**

Each book, each system, each framework becomes a Git repository of knowledge that people can:
- Add to
- Edit
- Fork for their own use

These become **alignment nodes** - shared frameworks for how to align AI assistance.

**The invitation:**

neoMeta is me sharing what I believe I'm going to align MY AI with. I invite you to:
- Use what resonates
- Comment and help improve it
- Build your own version
- Contribute back

## FRAME 4: INTEGRITY AS OPERATING SYSTEM

### Section: The Meta-Principle

### The Discovery

> Integrity is not one pillar among many. It's the operating system that makes all pillars function.

Without integrity, optimization is efficiently moving in the wrong direction. You can have perfect health habits, growing wealth, strong relationships. And still be misaligned with your own values.

### Three Layers of Integrity

**1. Truthfulness**
Not speaking words that are not true. Not afraid to own actions. Becoming a truth-worshiper. Honoring reality over falsehood.

**2. Vulnerability**
Awareness of what's happening internally, then expressing and sharing it. Can't be confident in alignment if not aware of the different layers.

**3. Impeccability**
Alignment of actions with chosen values, beliefs, faith. Don Miguel Ruiz's first agreement: be impeccable with your word.

### Why Awareness First

> "Integrity starts with awareness. We cannot bring into coherence and align all our different layers if those layers themselves are not in our awareness."

Before we can:
- Align words with truth
- Align actions with values
- Align external expression with internal reality

We must first align awareness with actual experience in this moment.

This is why Meditate undergirds everything.

### The Neuroscience

**Metabolic Cost of Deception:**
- Brain uses 20% of body's energy while being 2% of mass
- Lying requires: inhibitory control, creative processing, theory of mind, working memory
- fMRI shows lying activates prefrontal cortex far more than truth-telling
- The "background program" of deception drains mental battery

**The Science of Honesty Study (Notre Dame):**
- "No-lie" group showed significant reduction in health complaints
- 3 fewer white lies per week = 4 fewer mental health complaints + 3 fewer physical complaints

### The Philosophical Foundation

**Plato's Leaky Jar:** The hedonist's soul leaks. No matter how much pleasure poured in, it immediately drains out. The integrated person retains their contents.

**Aristotle's Function Argument:** We cannot flourish if our character is fragmented. Integrity is the sine qua non of the good life.

**The Stoic Fortress:** Virtue is the only good. A tyrant can take property, reputation, life. But cannot force violation of integrity.

## FRAME 5: THE INTERNAL REVOLUTION THESIS

### Section: The Foundational Principle

### The Core Insight

> "Our best chance of having the best life and feeling fulfilled and inspired comes from changing ourselves, not from changing circumstances."

This means:
- Taking responsibility
- Realizing we can choose our state despite circumstances
- Learning to enjoy what we have instead of waiting to get what we think we need
- Changing perspective rather than chasing external change

### The Projection Thesis

Our current state determines what we see. If we see pain externally, we have pain inside. Internal pain projects into experience and validates itself.

**Implication:** A lot of our attention and energy should be aimed at improving our state of being. Because:
- When we change our state, we see the world differently
- We cause a different reality
- We notice the opportunity
- We find the gold

### The Sequence

1. **State** (internal condition)
2. **Perception** (what we notice/interpret)
3. **Reality** (the world we experience)
4. **Action** (choices we make)
5. **Feedback** (reinforces state)

Breaking the negative loop requires intervening at the state level - which is exactly what the Core domains (Health, Wealth, Connection) address.

### What We're Actually Chasing

> "We're not chasing the new life, the new car, the achievement. We're chasing what we believe these things will do to how we feel."

Underneath every desire is the belief it will make us feel:
- More energized
- More peaceful
- More connected
- More meaningful
- More fulfilled

**The insight:** We can cultivate these states directly. Without waiting for external circumstances to deliver them.
