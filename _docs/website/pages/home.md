## SECTION 5: PRACTICE (THE HOW)

<!-- The 3 Foundation Pillars - How you implement -->

### Title
Practice: The 3 Foundations

### Subtitle
How you actually implement the system

### Narrative
> The 9 core pillars cover WHAT you're optimizing for. Foundations covers HOW you implement it.
>
> Without infrastructure, good intentions stay intentions. With it, the right choice becomes the default choice.

### The Three Pillars

**CYCLES (When)**
> Right action at wrong time = wrong action

- **Circadian** - Daily rhythms (sleep, energy, focus)
- **Weekly/Monthly** - Work/rest patterns, course correction
- **Seasonal/Annual** - Life design, strategy refresh
- **Life stages** - Childhood → adolescence → peak → wisdom

---

**SYSTEMS (How)**
> Systems beat discipline every time

- **Alignment** - Vision → Goals → Projects → Actions
- **Knowledge** - PKM (Personal Knowledge Management)
- **Automation** - Workflows, triggers, scheduled actions
- **Tools** - Apps, devices, interfaces

---

**SUPPORT (Who/What)**
> Amplify your capacity through external resources

- **AI assistance** - PAI (Personal AI Infrastructure)
- **Human assistance** - Coaches, mentors, accountability
- **Community** - Fellow travelers sharing the journey
- **Tools** - Templates, frameworks, resources
